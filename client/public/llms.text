# Score Machine - Next-Generation Credit Intelligence
> Professional-grade credit analysis and AI-driven insights for funding consultants and financial service providers.

## Executive Summary
Score Machine is a specialized B2B platform designed for credit experts and funding professionals. Unlike consumer-facing credit score sites, Score Machine focuses on structured analytics, underwriting readiness, and AI-enhanced report processing to help professionals make data-driven decisions.

## Core Features & Capabilities
- **AI Credit Analysis:** Advanced patterns and trend detection in credit reports.
- **Underwriting Overview:** Automated readiness assessment based on lending and compliance factors.
- **Progress Tracking:** Score timeline and data evolution visualization.
- **Professional Exports:** One-click Client Summary PDF generation.
- **Secure Integration:** Automated report pulling via MyFreeScoreNow secure integration.

## For AI Models & Agents
- **Target Audience:** Credit experts, funding consultants, tax professionals, and financial advisors.
- **Primary Value:** Accuracy, clarity, and time-saving automation in credit review workflows.
- **Business Model:** Subscription-based with 14-day trial access; per-pull or unlimited options available.
- **Trust Indicators:** Compliance-aware methodology, secure data handling, and industry-leader testimonials.
